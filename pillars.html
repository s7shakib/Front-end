Five Pillars of Islam

In the religion of Islam there is five pillars, and they are obligatory on each Muslim.

    Five Pillars

  1. Profession of Faith (shahada)
  2. Prayer (salat)
  3. Alms (Zakat)
  4. Fasting (sawm)
  5. Pilgrimage (Hajj)
